FAQ

What is StrikeZone Kickboxing?

StrikeZone Kickboxing is a martial arts gym that offers kickboxing classes for all levels.

Do I need to have prior experience?

No, you don't need to have prior experience. Our classes are designed for all levels, from beginners to advanced.

What should I wear to class?

We recommend wearing comfortable clothing and bringing a water bottle. You can also wear a mouthguard if you have one.
